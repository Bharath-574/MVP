Waste Management Guide for Citizens

🗂️ Waste Segregation

🟢 Wet Waste
  • • Kitchen scraps
  • • Food leftovers
  • • Vegetable peels
  • • Garden waste
🔵 Dry Waste
  • • Paper & cardboard
  • • Plastic bottles
  • • Metal cans
  • • Glass items
🔴 Hazardous Waste
  • • Batteries
  • • Electronics
  • • Medical waste
  • • Chemicals

💡 Best Practices

  • ✅ Use separate bins for different waste types
  • ✅ Clean containers before disposal
  • ✅ Compost organic waste at home
  • ✅ Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • ❌ Don't mix wet and dry waste
  • ❌ Avoid single-use plastics
  • ❌ Don't burn waste materials
  • ❌ Never litter in public spaces

🇮🇳 Swachh Bharat Mission

The Swachh Bharat Mission is a nationwide campaign launched by the Government of India to eliminate open defecation and improve solid waste management. As responsible citizens, we play a crucial role in making India clean and healthy.

Mission Objectives:
  • • Achieve universal sanitation coverage
  • • Eliminate open defecation
  • • Eradicate manual scavenging
  • • 100% scientific solid waste management
  • • Generate awareness about sanitation
Citizen's Role:
  • • Practice proper waste segregation
  • • Use public toilets responsibly
  • • Participate in cleanliness drives
  • • Spread awareness in community
  • • Report sanitation issues
