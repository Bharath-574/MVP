Employee Benefits & Schemes

The Government of India has introduced several schemes to improve the safety, dignity, and livelihood of sanitation workers, including landfill operators. These initiatives aim to formalize the workforce and provide a crucial social security net.

  • NAMASTE Scheme: This flagship program provides health insurance under the **Ayushman Bharat-PMJAY** scheme, occupational safety training, and access to safety equipment.
  • Financial Inclusion: Workers are being integrated into formal financial systems with access to bank accounts, loans, and other financial services.
  • Pension Schemes: Various pension schemes like **Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Yojana** offer a financial safety net for old age.
  • PPE and Safety: Provision of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kits, including gloves, masks, and safety shoes, to ensure a safer working environment.
